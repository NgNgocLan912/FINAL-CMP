Our Story

Amore" is a charming French bistro that transports diners to the streets of Paris. The restaurant's story is one of love and nostalgia. So we decided to bring a piece of Paris to Hanoi.

Amore" is a cozy and romantic restaurant with a menu featuring classic French dishes like Escargot, Coq au Vin, and Ratatouille. The owner, who is often seen chatting with guests, has carefully curated a selection of French wines to complement the culinary experience. The restaurant's interior is adorned with vintage Parisian decor, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

One of the restaurant's standout features is its delightful courtyard garden, where diners can enjoy their meals in a tranquil setting, shielded from the hustle and bustle of the city. The garden is adorned with fairy lights, making it a popular spot for couples and those seeking a romantic dinner under the stars.

Amore" in Hanoi is not just a restaurant; it's a place where Vietnamese and French cultures merge to create a truly authentic dining experience. It's a testament to the enduring connection between Vietnam and France, and it's a must-visit for anyone seeking a taste of Europe in the heart of Hanoi.
